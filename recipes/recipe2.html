Chicken Tikka Masala

A flavorful Indian dish with marinated chicken in a creamy tomato sauce.


Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 200g plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tikka masala spice mix
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 200ml coconut milk or heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked basmati rice or naan bread for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, tikka masala spice mix, salt, and pepper. Add chicken cubes and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, cooking for another minute.
  4. Add the marinated chicken to the pan and cook until browned on all sides.
  5. Stir in canned tomatoes and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Pour in coconut milk or heavy cream, stirring well. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh cilantro.
  8. Serve hot with basmati rice or naan bread.

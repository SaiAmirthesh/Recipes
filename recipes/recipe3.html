Vegetable Stir Fry

A quick and healthy stir fry with mixed vegetables in a savory sauce.




Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water (optional for thickening)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving
  • Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish (optional)


Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, saut√©ing for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  4. Pour in the soy sauce and stir well to coat the vegetables.
  5. If using, add the cornstarch mixture to thicken the sauce, stirring until it reaches your desired consistency.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot over cooked rice or noodles, garnished with sesame seeds and green onions if desired.
